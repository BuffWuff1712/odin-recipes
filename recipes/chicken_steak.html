Chicken Steak

Description

Chicken Steak is a pan-seared or grilled chicken breast marinated and served with a savoury sauce and sides like mashed potatoes or vegetables. It’s a versatile dish perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic and thyme
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Vegetables or sides

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, garlic, thyme, and lemon juice.
  2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium-high heat
  4. Cook the chicken for 5–6 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Let it rest briefly before slicing and serving with sides.
