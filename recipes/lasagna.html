Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese. It’s baked until golden and bubbling, offering a rich and hearty meal that’s perfect for gatherings or comfort food nights.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook ground beef with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it rest before slicing and serving.
